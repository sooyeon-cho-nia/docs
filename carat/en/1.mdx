---
title: "How do I cancel my paid subscription?"
---

The cancellation method depends on how you subscribed.

**If you subscribed through the app:**

- **Google Play Store**: Open the Play Store app \> Tap your profile icon \> Payments & subscriptions \> Subscriptions
- **Apple App Store**: Open the Settings app \> Tap your Apple ID \> Subscriptions

**If you subscribed on the web:**

- Visit the [Carat website](https://carat.im) \> Click your username at the bottom right \> Settings \> Manage Subscription \> Cancel Plan

Once canceled, you won’t be charged again. You can still enjoy your subscription benefits until the end of your current billing period.
